What is Hackatron?

Hackatron is a 24 hours Hackathon and is attempt made by the Hackathon and Coding Club (HnCC), BIT Sindri to bring out the developer hidden inside a student, by providing a platform to showcase the skills and stand out of the crowd.

MEET DOGE,OUR MASCOT

Meet Doge. He is a hacker. He is lazy. Oh, Wait! Don't think of him as some ordinary sloth. He learns technical stuff by hacking i.e exploring and learning on the go. He will help you throughout the hackatron, in case you stumble upon any problem. Ask Doge anything without any hesitance.
